Why Ethik?

Our products are water proof, thus they last for a longer duration. Being allergy free and retains its shape for longer duration.

Not only that, our innovative production ensure that the visual appeal of leather is maintained, but all the while reducing wastage and being Ethik-al.

This is the vanguard
which saves lives.

Our products are what you call "ethical". Not only are they leather-free, but they give you advantages which you won't get anywhere else. How do we do it? Using NEATHER.

NEATHER as a product is one which has immense tensile strength, it gives a brilliant look and feel to any fashion accessory be it shoes, belts, wallets etc. In addition it is highly durable and comfortable, water proof, allergy free and retains its shape for a longer duration.

The best part about it is that, it gives you a feeling of joy. The joy that fills your heart as you know you are adorning a green product, a product that has considered the environment and everything it holds as a friend.
